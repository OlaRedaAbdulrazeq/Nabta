Flowers Care Instructions:
  • Replenish the water frequently.
  • Change the water entirely every 2-3 days.
  • Trim at least a half inch of stem before putting in a vase and each time you change water.
  • Keep away from heat and bright light.
  • Avoid ripening fruit, especially bananas and apples.
  • Use sharp scissors when cutting.

Note: Flowers can last up to 7 days if the above care instructions are followed and well maintained.
